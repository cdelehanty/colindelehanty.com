    ## Practical Shoot

</MDXContainer>

<MDXContainer containerType="text">
	Obscura developed a permanent projection system that transforms The Mart in
	Chicago into a large-scale illuminated canvas, showcasing visual artistry five
	nights a week, ten months a year, for the next 30 years. Content from this
	practical FX shoot was featured alongside works by four curated international
	artists as part of the inaugural show.

    Using templates of The Mart’s façade as a creative guide, Obscura’s media team
    produced abstract visuals for the installation through practical visual
    effects techniques. As the Practical FX Director, I oversaw all aspects of
    cinematography, rigging, and lighting during the three-day shoot, ensuring
    each element aligned with the building's architectural features and the
    artistic vision of the project.

</MDXContainer>

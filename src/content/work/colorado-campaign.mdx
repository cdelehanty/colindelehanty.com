    ## Summer Campaign

</MDXContainer>

<MDXContainer containerType="text">
	Together with Forge Motion pictures, Camp 4 Collective shot a series of nine "Come to Life"
	television spots promoting Colorado tourism. I joined this project for a five day shoot beginning
	in Grand Junction and ending in Steamboat Springs with the goal of capturing time-lapses from each
	of the 7 shoot locations in Colorado. This was a fast moving production so I operated independetly
	of the primary film crew planning shots and meeting times remotely. In total I shot, edited and
	delivered 27 time-lapses from 7 locations in 5 days.
</MDXContainer>
